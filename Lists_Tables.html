Jollof Rice Recipe

This recipe helps you make the best jollof rice in the world.

You will need the following items to make your jollof rice:

  • Cooking Utensils
  • Rice
  • Tomatoe paste
  • Seasoning
  • Blended Pepper
  • Curry and Thyme
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Water

Follow the following procedures to make your jollof rice:

  1. pour water into the pot
  2. put content on heating system
  3. put your rice into pot of water and parboil for 15mins
  4. wash the rice with clean water
  5. wash and spice your chicken and parboil till tender
  6. chop your onion and set out your other ingrdients on the table
  7. put a clean pot on the stove and pour in your cooking oil
  8. Add your chopped onion, tin tomatoes, blended pepper, seasoning,curry and thyme and fry for 10 mins
  9. Add your cicken stock into the pot, add small amount of water too and allow it to boil for 5 mins
  10. Add your rice into the poyt and make sure the water is a little above the rice
  11. Add your salt for taste
  12. Allow the rice to boil for 25-30 mins
  13. Your jollof rice is ready.
