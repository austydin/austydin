WHO IS OHALELE AUGUSTINA CHIDINMA?

A LOVER OF GOD, FASHION DESIGNER, CATERER AND A WEB DEVELOPER

BIOGRAPHY

OHALELE AUGUSTINA CHIDINMA also known as "TINA"was born in Lagos State Nigeria in the year 1960. She is a christian who hails from Imo State. She attended St Mary's College where she obtained her 'O'Level. She is a lover of God, Fashion Designer,Caterer And a Web Developer.

She obtained her first degree from the Federal Polytechnic Nekede Owerri.
